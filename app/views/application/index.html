Booking economy no longer means booking with restrictions

Theia is a marketplace for previously issued air tickets.

We connect price-sensitive air passengers and facilitate the efficient reassignment of tickets issued by participating airlines.

Check out how Theia works

Can't or don't want to fly on that particular booking?

Booked passengers that are not able to travel due to unforeseen events, or simply decide not to travel, can sell their tickets on the platform, recovering a significant proportion of the value paid for the original ticket and airline fees, in cash.

Looking for cheaper air tickets?

Passengers using the platform are able to buy previously issued air tickets at a discount to the current airfare.

Because they buy pre-owned tickets, they buy them cheaper, always.

Win-Win (All figures are real)

Paula paid £237.85 to fly from Bordeaux to Tenerife with Spanish airline Volotea. Now she has to cancel the trip. If Paula decides to fly at some later date, she will have to pay £85.97 in airline fees (36% additional cost). Paula is likely to let the ticket expire (now or later), losing £237.85.

But she now posts the ticket on Theia and finds Alex, who is willing to pay pay £239.86 for Paula’s ticket (airfare has gone up to £266.51 since Paula booked it). Paula sells the ticket and recovers 67% of the original ticket and fees, after transaction costs... and Alex bought it at a 10% discount to the current airline price.

Alex is now the passenger on record and travels like any other passenger. Paula has been refunded so she can spend the money as she wishes, without restrictions. Win-Win
